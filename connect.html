Enterprise Connect

Enterprise Connect provides the comprehensive analysis and in-depth information you need to do your job, providing expert insight across topics such as Unified Communications, SIP Trunking, Cloud, Video, Speech Technologies, Team Collaboration, WebRTC, and more.

PROGRAM IS JUST ABOUT COMPLETE

We’ve got all but a handful of our breakout sessions posted on the website. We’re trying to make our Conference Program page more useful by adding a feature that you can use to sort the sessions by Hot Topics as well as by the Conference Track that they’re in. The 9 Conference Tracks cover the biggest trends and issues—UC&C, Contact Centers, Video, etc.—while the Hot Topics are areas that didn’t warrant a whole track, but do appear throughout the program, often scattered across different Tracks. So for example, if you sort by the Hot Topic on Artificial Intelligence (AI), you’ll get a list of the sessions that address AI’s emerging role in communications and collaboration—some of those sessions might be in the Contact Center Track, others in our new Track on Speech Technologies, etc. Hopefully this feature will help you find topics, especially in the emerging tech area, that touch different aspects of enterprise communications in different ways.

MEET OUR ADVISORY BOARD

One of the things we’ve done to add our attendees’ voice to the planning process has been to create an Advisory Board made up of 8 individuals from enterprise communications/IT organizations. These folks have generously agreed to share their insights with our Program Committee on a quarterly basis, to help us deepen our understanding of trends in the industry, how their jobs are evolving, and what they’d like to see at the show. I want to express my gratitude here to these professionals for their willingness to help us out—they’re the kind of smart, wise, great people that make working on Enterprise Connect a pleasure.
